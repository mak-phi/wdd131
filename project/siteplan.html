Overview

Site Name

"pWeza Knowledge Verse"

"Pweza" refers to octopus in Kiswahili. Octopuses are incredibly curious. I wanted the name to inspire learners to be curious and seek out knowledge wherever it might be found. On the other hand "weza" means to be able. The aim is to encourage learners to believe in themselves and foster a growth mindset.

Site Purpose

To provide access to various learning media (resources) - including audio, video, document, webpages, images and articles - that support learning, particularly for home-schooled learners, from Kindergarten to Grade 12

To provide a platform for teachers or parents to interact, share teaching ideas and support each other. This platform will also enable learners to share their experiences, ask various topic related questions and learn from each other.

Scenarios

  • Where can I read an article about dinosaurs and see images of therizinosaurus and ankylosaurus?
  • Which videos can I use to help me illustrate how algebra is usfeul in everyday life to my 5th grade class?
  • Where can I find supplementary questions to ask my 7th grade home-schooled daughter to test her understanding of the human circulatory system?
  • What interesting music audio can I have my 6th graders listen to and write an essay on?
  • Where can I find video tutorials that can teach me how to do a running stitch?
